# The Imitation

**Header:**
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LE3EplcP6UE

**Status:** Shipped

## Information

**Platform:** PC, VR
**Genre:** Horror

First-person survival horror where creatures that mimic exhibits stalk you in a museum. Your only defense: observation and a hand-cranked flashlight.

### Guide

- The statues will attempt to attack you. Shine your flashlight on them and keep looking at them to freeze them in place.
- Repair the electrical door systems to progress through the museum's different rooms.

**Technical Info**
- Unity 6000 / OpenXR
- Global Game Jam 2026
